THE CHILDREN MINISTRY

We are passionate about children and the younger generation. We labour to impart the essence of Christ in the heart of our children and young infants

Our Commitment

The Children Department of Foursquare Jebako has been a core sensitive ministry of the Church since inception. We appreciate the value of raising godly children, mentored to change and influence their world.

To the Glory of God, our standards haven't changed. We are very much involved in imparting our children with all godly knowledge, making them equipped and important to the society and the word at large.

How we Operate

We operate a typical class-based lesson with regular promotional tests.

Young infants are catered for separately in the daycare while older children are grouped into age-specific classes. Promotion is seasonal and test based, until they are mature enough to be promoted to the Teenagers department.

We love to see our children mature and mature rightly.
